About Covid-19

What's COVID-19?

Coronavirus disease 2019 [COVID-19] is the illness caused by the novel virus of the coronavirus family. The predecessors of this virus were SARS and MERS which dominated the epidamic senario of the early 21st century. Covid-19 has significantly lower mortality rates when compared to its predecessors but yet is much more contagious. Social distancing and frequent washing of hands with soap and water are the only effective measures that one can take in the absence of a vaccine against it.

Covid-19 Symptoms

What are the symptoms?

Coronavirus has an incubation period of 5-14 days. Symptoms of Covid-19 may include but are not limited to:

  • Coughing
  • Head ache
  • High Fever
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness in Breath
  • Perplexity

Covid-19 Spread

How does it spread?

According to health experts Novel Coronavirus spreads through:

  • Respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes.
  • Coming in contact with unidentified and unsanitised articles or objects.
